Hi there!

My name is Iordan

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computational Geometry, member of the research group GAMBLE in Nancy, France. I have the privilege of working with Monique Teillaud on the exciting topic Triangulations of Hyperbolic Manifolds. The goal of my thesis is to formulate (both theoretically and practically) a robust and efficient method for computing Delaunay triangulations of 2D hyperbolic manifolds. Implementation will be inspired by and based on CGAL.

I hold a Master's degree in Applied and Computational Mathematics from the University of Crete, Greece, as well as a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics from the same institution.
